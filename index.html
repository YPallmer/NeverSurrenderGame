Never Surrender Game Manual

How to play the game?

Controls:

up_arrow = jump.

Left and Righr arrow = moving left and right.

Down_arrow = crouch.

Q_Key_on_keyboard = punch

E_key_on_Keyboard = kick

Game tips:

Move back to block enemy upcoming attack

Press_down_arrow and Opposite direction to block low attacks

Combos:

down,left + punch = fireball

down,left + kick = Special attack
